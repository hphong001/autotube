The Automation Pipeline

How AutoTube Pro
Streamlines Your Workflow

Click start and walk away. Dozens of media assets are generated sequentially and synced perfectly without human intervention.

1
STEP 1 — Prep your script in Excel

Use your favorite AI (ChatGPT, Claude) to write a script and paste it into our standardized Excel template. This is the single source of truth for the entire engine.

2
STEP 2 — Batch Generate Images

The system reads your Excel prompts and generates visuals sequentially. For a 60-cut video, 60 images are fully rendered and saved hands-free.

3
STEP 3 — Batch Generate Voiceovers (TTS)

Your script lines are sent to the local AI voice engine. It renders individual audio files for each cut and merges them into a master track with natural pacing.

4
STEP 4 — Whisper Subtitle Extraction

The master audio is analyzed using precise AI transcription to automatically calculate the exact start and end timecodes for every single word.

5
STEP 5 — Instant CapCut Assembly

With a single click, all generated images, the master audio track, and timed subtitles are injected directly into a native CapCut project file in 1 second. Just open CapCut, add your music, and export.
